The Story of Moosey and Friends

It all started when I was a young lad, at my grandparent’s house. I was gifted a stuffed animal moose, which I aptly named Moosey. I cherished it and kept it for years, until, somehow, I had gathered 12 friends for it. With 13 stuffed moose in my possession, I thought, “Why not just officially start a collection?”

With that, it began. I labeled the 13 I had as founders, then every thrift store trip and every birthday or Christmas gift made it grow. Each one got a name and a number in the order I got it.

I kept going until about 2021, when with my 113th moose, I declared the collection finished (then I got one more as a gift, but that doesn’t really count).

Today, they live in my hometown bedroom, completely filling two bookshelves. While at home, every week I would put a different one on my desk as decoration. Now they can live on forever, immortalized in pixels.
